# FLEXUS 5-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00-0:30 Problem

Bengaluru authorities deal with recurring road incidents, breakdowns, potholes, construction disruptions, water logging, public events, and closure-prone areas. The available data is not traffic sensor data, so FLEXUS focuses on incident intelligence rather than fake congestion simulation.

## 0:30-1:15 Dataset Reality

Show the Dataset Explorer. Explain that the data contains event type, cause, coordinates, timestamps, priority, road closure flag, corridor, police station, status, description, and vehicle information. State clearly that speed, volume, density, road capacity, and live sensor feeds are not available.

## 1:15-2:00 Executive Overview

Show incident counts, high-priority share, closure share, top causes, top corridors, and the incident map. Emphasize that FLEXUS turns raw event logs into operational intelligence.

## 2:00-2:45 Event DNA

Open Event DNA Explorer. Explain that incidents are clustered using structured fields, location, time, priority, closure flag, vehicle type, and text descriptions. Show cluster summaries and examples.

## 2:45-3:30 Hotspot Intelligence

Open Hotspot Intelligence. Show risk-ranked hotspots and explain that DBSCAN identifies recurring spatial incident clusters.

## 3:30-4:10 Risk And Resilience

Open Incident Risk Predictor and Resilience Score Dashboard. Explain that the model predicts incident risk proxies such as closure likelihood, not traffic flow. Show corridor resilience rankings.

## 4:10-4:45 Recommendations

Open Recommendations Center. Show rule-based recommendations derived from hotspots, recurring causes, closure rates, and low-resilience areas.

## 4:45-5:00 Close

FLEXUS is realistic because it respects the dataset. It avoids hallucinated traffic data and delivers a working local platform for incident intelligence, monitoring prioritization, and operational decision support.
