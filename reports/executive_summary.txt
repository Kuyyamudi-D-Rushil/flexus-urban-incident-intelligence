Executive Summary
=================

Dataset
-------
8,173 usable incident records from Astram event data_anonymized - Astram event data_anonymizedb40ac87.csv.

What FLEXUS Does
----------------
FLEXUS analyzes urban incident patterns, identifies hotspots, clusters similar event DNA, estimates incident risk, scores corridor resilience, and generates rule-based operational recommendations.

What FLEXUS Does Not Do
-----------------------
It does not predict traffic speed, volume, density, routes, or congestion propagation because those columns are not present in the dataset.
